Revolutionizing Brain Tumor Detection with AI: The Power of ResNet and ResUnet

Our AI model is designed to accurately detect and classify brain tumors using state-of-the-art techniques. The model first uses a ResNet (Residual Network) to classify medical images, such as MRI or CT scans, as either containing a tumor or being tumor-free. ResNet is a type of deep convolutional neural network that allows for the effective training of deep models and has shown great success in image classification tasks. If a tumor is detected, the model then uses a ResUnet (Residual U-Net) to accurately segment and locate the tumor in the image. ResUnet is a type of deep learning model that combines the features of U-Net and ResNet to provide improved performance in medical image segmentation tasks. The combination of these two state-of-the-art techniques results in a highly efficient and accurate system for the diagnosis of brain tumors. By reducing the potential for misdiagnosis and streamlining the diagnostic process, our AI model has the potential to revolutionize the field of brain tumor detection and classification.
